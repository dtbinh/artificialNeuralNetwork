Infotext:
This app visualises how an artificial neural network is trained. (By backpropagation if you care)

First choose the dimensions of the network. (The higher the total number of neurons, the longer the training will be.)

Then choose the input of the network and the result you want to obtain.
(To avoid endless execution the training stops after 1000 trials so it might happen no result will be found.)
